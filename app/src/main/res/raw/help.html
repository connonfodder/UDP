How to use?
  1. Login with default account: manager/123456
  2. Before starting taking order, you need to setup tables, categories, items and modifiers for your restaurant in setting.
  3. For receipt printing, you have to setup printer in setting menu. Make sure correct printer setting.
  4. After setting, you can open table, take orders, and send orders and kitchen/bar printout.
  5. After sent orders, you can check orders by long click table, close orders and print receipt.
Tables
  1. Dining table in color
    1. Click table to menu screen.
  2. Free table
    1. Click table to open table.
  3. Click device menu button, to report, setting, database.
Ordering
  1. Click ordering item name to change price, add remark and holding.
  2. Click "Confirm" to confirm order items.
  3. Click "+" to add order item.
  4. Click "-" to reduce order item.
  5. Click "green box" to add modifiers.
Ordered
  1. Shows order,items, payment information for the specific table.
  2. Click order item to reprint, void, transfer and change price.
  3. Click "Transfer" button to Transfer order to open/free table.
  4. Click "Split" button split order in multiple bills by amount or item.
  5. Click "Person" button to update number of person.
Payment
  1. Clearly show subtoal, discount, gratuity, tax, total, paid and change.
  2. Click "Print" to print receipt.
  3. Click "Drawer" to open cash drawer.
  4. Click "Customer" to add customer on order.
  5. Click "Email" to email receipt.
  6. Click "Note" to add note on receipt.
  7. Click "Exactly" for the paid as same total.
