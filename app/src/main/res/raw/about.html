W&O Restaurant POS is a full featured, easy to use, fully stand-alone POS for small and medium scale business owners, specially for restaurant, bar and coffee shop etc. You just need to download, install, and use without a complicated back-end server, compatible with your existing hardware (printer and drawer). W&O POS Restaurant saves your money and time.

This version has most functions without advertising, please upgrade/purchase advance features to support our improving works.

Ordering
• Open/close table
• Void/transfer/split order
• Take out/dinning in order
• Wait/Cook order item

Menu
• Create/modify/delete table, category, item and supplement
• Import/export/ordering table, category, item and supplement
• Two taxes on items
• Include/Exclude tax on item price
• Kitchen/bar printers on items
• Price schedule on items
• Share menu with multiple devices by Dropbox™

Printer
• Support most thermal receipt printers with LAN connection and ESC/POS Command
• Cash drawer(RJ12)
